# Ideation Cheat Sheet

*A facilitator's cue card — keep this open during ideation. One printable page.*

---

## ☐ Before You Start (2 min setup)

- [ ] Timer visible to everyone (phone, projector, wall clock)
- [ ] BRIEF.md projected or printed — everyone can see it
- [ ] Sticky notes + markers out (physical) or Miro board open (virtual)
- [ ] 3-5 seed "What if..." hypotheses ready to show
- [ ] Feasibility scorecard projected (for later)

---

## 🔥 The Spark — Opening Ritual (10 min)

Start with energy, not slides.

1. **Customer war story** (3 min) — Ask the sponsor: *"Tell us about the last time [pain point] cost you real time or money."* Let them talk. This grounds everything.
2. **The question** (2 min) — Facilitator reframes the war story: *"What if we could [outcome] in [timeframe]?"*
3. **Ground rules** (1 min) — "No idea is bad. Time-boxes are sacred. Feasibility comes later. The customer's voice matters most."
4. **Reveal seeds** (2 min) — Show the 3-5 hypothesis canvases. "These are conversation starters, not answers."

> 💡 **Energy tip:** Stand up. Make eye contact. Your energy sets the room's energy.

---

## ✍️ Silent Write (8 min — timer visible)

⏱️ **Strictly enforced.** No talking. No exceptions.

Everyone writes use case ideas — one idea per sticky note / canvas card. Format: *"What if [persona] could [outcome] by [approach]?"*

Target: 2-3 ideas per person.

> 💡 **Energy tip:** Play ambient focus music (lo-fi, no lyrics). Walk the room silently — your presence keeps people writing.

> ⚠️ **Why this matters:** Without silence, the loudest voice dominates. You lose the best ideas from quieter participants and the customer's own thinking.

---

## 🗣️ Round Robin (2 min per person)

Go around the table. Each person:
- Reads their sticky notes aloud (30 sec each)
- No debate, no feedback — just listen
- Facilitator sticks notes on the wall / drops in Miro

> 💡 **Energy tip:** Thank each person after their turn. "Love it — next!" keeps momentum.

> **If >8 people:** Split into two groups for this phase, merge for clustering.

---

## 🧩 Cluster & Vote (15 min)

### Cluster (5 min)
Group similar ideas together on the wall. Name each cluster with a sticky note header.

### Discuss (5 min)
Open discussion on top clusters — clarify, combine, refine. **Customer input is gold here.**

### Dot vote (5 min)
- Each person gets **3 dots** (stickers or virtual votes)
- Vote for **highest customer value** — not what's technically coolest
- Silent voting — no lobbying
- Customer votes count extra (informally — watch where their dots land)

> 💡 **Energy tip:** Make voting physical and visible. Walk up to the wall. Slap the dot. It builds commitment.

---

## 📊 Feasibility Scoring (10 min per use case)

Take the top 4-6 voted use cases. Score each one together as a group.

| Dimension | 🟢 1 | 🟡 2 | 🔴 3 |
|-----------|-------|-------|-------|
| Data Availability | Exists, accessible | Needs discovery | No data or blocked |
| Data Quality | Clean, structured | Needs cleaning | Messy, insufficient |
| Integration Count | 0-1 systems | 2-3 systems | 4+ systems |
| Model Complexity | Simple rules | Moderate custom | Research-grade |
| Visualization | Standard charts | Interactive dash | Real-time / novel |
| Auth / Security | No special needs | RBAC or masking | Compliance review |

**Bands:** 6-8 = 🟢 Go · 9-12 = 🟡 Go with scope cut · 13-15 = 🟠 Stretch · 16-18 = 🔴 Redirect

The **Architect** leads scoring. The **customer** answers. Facilitator keeps time.

> 💡 **Energy tip:** Scoring can feel heavy. Keep it conversational: *"Given what we know right now, where does this land?"* Not a jury deliberation.

---

## ✊ The Commit (5 min)

1. Pick **1-3 use cases** to build (max 3 for 2-day, max 2 for 1-day)
2. Assign each to a builder pairing
3. Confirm with the customer: *"We're going to build X, Y, and Z. Does that feel right?"*
4. **Announce it with energy.** This is the moment the hackathon becomes real.

> 💡 **Energy tip:** Have the customer say the names of what you're building. Their voice, their commitment, their hackathon.

---

## ⏱️ Time-Box Reference

| Phase | 1-Day | 2-Day |
|-------|-------|-------|
| The Spark | 10 min | 10 min |
| Silent Write | 8 min | 8 min |
| Round Robin | 10 min | 15 min |
| Cluster & Vote | 10 min | 15 min |
| Feasibility | 25 min | 40 min |
| The Commit | 5 min | 5 min |
| **Total** | **~1 hr 10 min** | **~1 hr 35 min** |

**Hard rule:** If ideation runs over, cut it — don't steal from build time.
