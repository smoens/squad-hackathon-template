# Feasibility Scorecard

*Score each use case across 6 dimensions. Lower is better — a score of 1 means "easy / no risk" and 3 means "hard / high risk".*

---

## Scoring Framework

| Dimension | 1 — 🟢 Green | 2 — 🟡 Yellow | 3 — 🔴 Red |
|-----------|--------------|---------------|------------|
| **Data Availability** | Data exists, accessible, documented | Data exists but needs discovery or access requests | No data, or blocked behind approvals |
| **Data Quality** | Clean, structured, ready to use | Needs cleaning, transformation, or enrichment | Messy, inconsistent, or insufficient volume |
| **Integration Count** | 0-1 systems to connect | 2-3 systems to connect | 4+ systems or complex orchestration |
| **Model / Logic Complexity** | Simple rules or off-the-shelf model | Moderate tuning or custom logic | Research-grade, no proven approach |
| **Visualization Needs** | Standard charts / tables | Interactive dashboard or custom UI | Real-time, 3D, or novel visualization |
| **Auth / Security Constraints** | No special requirements | Role-based access or data masking needed | Compliance review, encryption, or air-gapped |

---

## Assessment Bands

| Total Score | Assessment | Guidance |
|:-----------:|-----------|----------|
| **6 – 8** | 🟢 **Go** | Full steam — high confidence of demo-ready PoC |
| **9 – 12** | 🟡 **Go with scope cut** | Feasible but cut scope to the demo-able slice |
| **13 – 15** | 🟠 **Stretch** | Risky — only attempt if high customer value |
| **16 – 18** | 🔴 **Redirect** | Pivot to a different approach or defer entirely |
